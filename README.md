# Multi-Year Sales Activity Dashboard (2015-2017)

## 🎯 The Business Challenge
The sales team required a historical analysis of representative performance and regional market share to guide future targets and understand seasonal trends.

![Sales Activity Dashboard](99d9e426251e26d5843ed618f4673d12.png)

## 💡 Key Insights Discovered
* **Seasonal Volatility:** Identified a massive revenue surge peaking in **October**, allowing management to plan inventory for the Q4 transition.
* **Regional Dominance:** The **West Region** is the primary revenue driver, accounting for **45%** of total sales.
* **Top Performers:** Benchmarked 6 sales reps, with **Mike and Julie** leading performance (exceeding $200k each).
* **Product Strategy:** Differentiated between "volume drivers" (Bellen) and "revenue drivers" (Quad).

## 🛠️ Technical Skills Demonstrated
* **Trend Analysis:** Monthly sales tracking and seasonality identification.
* **Performance Benchmarking:** Ranking reps and products by Units vs. Revenue.
* **UI/UX Design:** Creating high-contrast, interactive dashboards.
